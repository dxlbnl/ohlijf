# Founder Personas & Brand Voice

The "About Us" content serves the dual purpose of building authority and displaying extreme empathy as "ervaringsdeskundigen" (experts by experience). 

> 🔗 **Source File:** [src/routes/(site)/ons/+page.md](file:///home/dexter/Projects/Web/ohlijf/src/routes/(site)/ons/+page.md)

## Robin Boelsums
- **Background**: Former HBO teacher, sustainability blogger.
- **The Struggle**: Suffered from non-stop headaches for 10 years, trying physical therapies, diets, and acupuncture to no avail.
- **The Turnaround**: Escaped the cycle in months by changing her psychological approach.
- **Relatability Angle**: Discusses her own ADHD and how the tendency to be "always on" (altijd 'aan' staan) drives physical symptoms. 

## Aurinke Stokx-Vos
- **Background**: Structural engineer (constructeur).
- **The Struggle**: A "bingo card" of physical ailments—migraines, concussions, and Long Covid.
- **The Turnaround**: Found a "new start" (Aurinke 2.0) by recognizing how her recently diagnosed ADHD interacted with her nervous system.

## The Brand Voice
The copy is highly empathetic, slightly rebellious against traditional medicine that dismisses chronic pain, but rigorously anchored in their "TU Delft" analytical background. It emphasizes lightness: *"We brengen het luchtig en met een glimlach, de stof is praktisch en behapbaar - want laten we eerlijk zijn, het is vaak al zwaar genoeg..."*
